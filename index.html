About Mysuru

Mysore situated in Karnataka is a land with rich historical past. It has many pre-historic sites and most of them are in the river valleys. The district possess excellent fauna and flora, rivers, hillocks, lakes and a moderate climate. The city with the majestic Mysore Palace, the royal mansions, public buildings, gardens, water bodies and planned markets exhibit an indelible impression of the vision of the Maharajas, their Dewans and other talented luminaries on the City Municipality as early as 1862.
